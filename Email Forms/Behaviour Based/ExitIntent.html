Design 32 · Exit-Intent

Move your cursor above
the viewport to trigger.

Exit-intent popups detect when a user's cursor moves toward the browser's address bar or tab — a reliable signal that they're about to leave the page.

This technique is widely used in newsletter and lead-capture workflows to recover abandoning visitors without interrupting active readers.

Move your mouse toward the top of the browser window to trigger the exit-intent popup. On mobile, tap the button below.
